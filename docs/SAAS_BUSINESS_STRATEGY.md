## üöß Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk 1: LLM Provider Outages**
- **Impact:** High (service unusable)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (happens 1-2x/year)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Automatic failover to alternative providers
  - Queue requests during outages
  - Real-time status page
  - SLA credits for downtime

**Risk 2: Unexpected LLM Cost Increases**
- **Impact:** High (margin compression)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (providers raise prices)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Pass 80% of increases to users (ToS)
  - Negotiate volume discounts early
  - Build cost buffer into pricing
  - Diversify across providers

**Risk 3: Security Breach**
- **Impact:** Critical (existential threat)
- **Likelihood:** Low (with proper security)
- **Mitigation:**
  - SOC 2 compliance from day 1
  - Regular penetration testing
  - Encrypt all data at rest/in transit
  - Cyber insurance policy

---

### Business Risks

**Risk 4: Low Customer Acquisition**
- **Impact:** High (no revenue)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (crowded market)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Aggressive content marketing (2 posts/week)
  - Referral program (viral loop)
  - Free tier to reduce friction
  - Double down on what works

**Risk 5: High Churn Rate**
- **Impact:** High (unsustainable growth)
- **Likelihood:** Low (sticky product)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Build conversation memory (lock-in)
  - Agent marketplace (network effects)
  - Proactive customer success
  - Annual plans (12-month commitment)

**Risk 6: Competitive Pressure**
- **Impact:** Medium (price wars)
- **Likelihood:** High (attracting competitors)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Build moats (data, network, integrations)
  - Focus on developer experience
  - Move fast, ship often
  - Strong brand identity

---

### Market Risks

**Risk 7: AI Regulation**
- **Impact:** High (compliance costs)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (EU AI Act, etc.)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Monitor regulatory developments
  - Build compliance features early
  - Geographic restrictions if needed
  - Legal counsel on retainer

**Risk 8: LLM Commoditization**
- **Impact:** Medium (reduced differentiation)
- **Likelihood:** High (models converging)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus on value-adds (agents, memory)
  - Build on top of commodities
  - Pivot to higher-level abstractions
  - Agent marketplace becomes primary value
